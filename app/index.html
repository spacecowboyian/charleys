OUR STORY

Charley's Garage is an exclusive Toyota repair shop. We are a small shop with a big passion for Toyota’s. We have been serving Boulder for 21 years, and have 100 combined years of Toyota experience.

We eat, sleep and breathe Toyota. After years of seeing what happens to Toyota's as they grow old, we can figure out just about anything! If not? It's a challenge we all invite! After all, we can use a nice challenge! Just doing the normal stuff can get a bit boring.
